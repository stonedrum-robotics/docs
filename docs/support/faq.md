# FAQ

**1. What is the difference between L20 and L20 Lite?**
The L20 Lite is lighter and more suited for educational purposes, while the L20 offers higher payload and industrial-grade linkage.

**2. Is ROS 1 supported?**
We officially support ROS 2. ROS 1 support is community-driven.

**3. How do I purchase a hand?**
Please contact us at info@stonedrum.co.
