# Hardware Setup

Follow these steps to safely unpack and connect your dexterous hand.

1. **Unboxing**: Carefully remove the hand from the Pelican case. Do not apply force to the fingers during unboxing.
2. **Mounting**: Use the provided adapter plate to mount the hand to your robot arm flange (e.g., ISO 9409-1-50-4-M6).
3. **Cabling**: Connect the power and communication cables (CAN/RS485).
4. **Power Supply**: Ensure the power supply is set to the correct voltage (24V or 48V depending on your model) before turning it on.

For detailed pinouts, refer to the [Hardware Section](../hardware/linkerbot-l20.md).
